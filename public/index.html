Explanation:

Our Living Quality Algorithm evaluates the environment based on several key parameters: hospitals, universities, police stations, schools, and parks. Each user type — whether you're a student, part of a family, or a pensionist — is assigned different weights to these parameters to reflect varying priorities.

Weights:
  • Students: Hospitals (0.6), Universities (0.8), Police Stations (0.4), Schools (0.3), Parks (0.6)
  • Families: Hospitals (0.8), Universities (0.5), Police Stations (0.6), Schools (0.7), Parks (0.7)
  • Pensionists: Hospitals (0.7), Universities (0.1), Police Stations (0.6), Schools (0.4), Parks (0.8)

The score is a weighted sum of the factors, normalized to a maximum of 3.

Ratings:
  • >= 3: Excellent!
  • >= 2: Good.
  • >= 1.5: Average.
  • < 1.5: Below Average.
